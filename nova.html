How To Play
The Idea
You are a glowing orb in space. Enemies swarm toward you from all directions.
Your only weapon is the Nova — a massive explosion that radiates outward from you.
Energy charges faster when enemies are closer to you. The more energy stored, the bigger the blast.
Let them get dangerously close, then detonate. Risk vs reward — that's the game.

Rules
You have 3 lives. If an enemy touches you, you lose one life.
Energy charges passively when enemies are within range. Closer = faster charging.
You need at least 10% energy to trigger a Nova. More energy = bigger explosion radius.
Killing 3+ enemies in one Nova triggers a chain bonus — more kills = exponentially more points.
Enemies come in waves. Clear all enemies in a wave to advance to the next.
Destroyed enemies may drop energy orbs (auto-collect nearby) or rare power-ups.
Score multiplier increases with wave number. Later waves = higher points per kill.
Lose all 3 lives and it's game over. Your best score is saved locally.
Enemy Types
Drone
Basic enemy. Moves straight toward you. 1 hit to destroy.
Seeker
Fast and small. Rushes you quickly. 1 hit to destroy.
Tank
Large and slow. Takes 3 hits. Shows a health bar when damaged.
Splitter
Splits into 2 smaller drones when destroyed. 2 hits to destroy.
Phaser
Zigzags unpredictably as it approaches. Hard to predict. 1 hit.
Power-Ups
Charge Boost
Energy charges 2.5x faster for 8 seconds
Nova Radius+
Explosion radius increases 1.8x for 10 seconds
Speed Boost
Move 1.6x faster for 6 seconds
Shield
Absorbs 1 enemy hit. Shown as a dashed ring around you.
Time Warp
Slows everything down, giving you time to react
HUD Guide
Top-left — Current wave number
Top-right — Your score
Bottom bar — Energy level. Color shifts from blue to red as it fills.
Bottom dots — Remaining lives (filled = alive, dim = lost)
Glowing aura around you — grows and changes color with stored energy
Red vignette at screen edges — danger! An enemy is very close.
Pro Tips
Don't nova at low energy — the blast will be tiny. Let it build up for devastating explosions.
Group enemies together by kiting them, then detonate for massive chain bonuses.
Splitters spawn 2 mini-drones on death — save some energy for the aftermath.
Energy orbs get pulled toward you automatically when you're close. Stay near the action.
When hit, all nearby enemies get knocked back — use that moment to reposition.
